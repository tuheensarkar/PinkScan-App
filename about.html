About PinkScan

PinkScan is a revolutionary AI-powered platform dedicated to early breast cancer detection, empowering individuals with cutting-edge technology and actionable insights. Our mission is to save lives through early intervention, accurate predictions, and personalized health recommendations.

Our Vision

We envision a world where breast cancer is detected early, managed effectively, and prevented through innovative technology and education. PinkScan aims to bridge the gap between medical expertise and everyday users, making health monitoring accessible to all. Our goals include:

  • Reducing breast cancer mortality rates through early detection.
  • Providing affordable, user-friendly tools for global communities.
  • Promoting awareness and education about breast health.
  • Collaborating with healthcare professionals to enhance outcomes.

Our Technology

Powered by a Random Forest Classifier trained on the Breast Cancer Wisconsin (Diagnostic) Dataset, PinkScan analyzes 30 key features to predict whether a tumor is malignant or benign. Our platform offers real-time analytics, downloadable reports, and multilingual support to cater to a global audience. Key aspects include:

  • Advanced machine learning models for high accuracy.
  • Interactive dashboards for user-friendly data visualization.
  • Secure, encrypted data handling to protect user privacy.
  • Integration with mobile and web platforms for accessibility.
  • Continuous updates based on the latest medical research.
